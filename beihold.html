Numb Little Bug

Artist

Em Beihold

Producer

Dru DeCaro, Elijah Hill, & Dallas Caton

*sound quality does not match purchased product.

Short Description

Beihold clams she wrote the single due to the pills she was taking for her anxiety, and how they made her feel “numb.” While she's thrilled that fans are telling her how much they relate to the song, she also believes it has negative commentary on our society - “Everyone expects me to be the happiest ever because the song is blowing up and I'm so grateful,” she explains, “but, I've been numb, because I don't know how to process it!” Although Beihold claims she no longer takes the initially mentioned medication, she admits she still feels a little like a “numb little bug.”
