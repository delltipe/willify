Short Description

"Satu-Satu" by Idgitaf explores themes of personal growth, resilience, and the journey of overcoming challenges one step at a time. The lyrics emphasize the importance of patience and perseverance, encouraging listeners to take things slowly and steadily ("satu-satu" translates to "one by one" in English). The song reflects on the process of healing and self-improvement, highlighting that progress often comes gradually. Through its introspective and hopeful message, "Satu-Satu" resonates with those facing difficulties and striving for a better future.
