Short Description

Released in 2020, the song captures the nostalgia and shared experiences of those who grew up in the 1990s. It's an anthem celebrating the cultural touchstones of the era, from iconic toys and games to memorable TV shows and fashion trends. The lyrics evoke a sense of camaraderie among "90s kids," reflecting on the innocence and simplicity of childhood during that time. With its catchy melody and relatable theme, "90s Kids" resonates with listeners who fondly remember the pop culture of the 1990s.
