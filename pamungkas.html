Short Description

"One Only" is a soulful and emotive track by Indonesian singer-songwriter Pamungkas, featured on his 2018 album Walk the Talk. The song delves into the themes of deep, enduring love and commitment, characterized by Pamungkas's heartfelt lyrics and soothing melodies. Its relatable lyrics and captivating tune have made it a favorite among listeners, highlighting Pamungkas's talent for conveying powerful emotions through his music.
