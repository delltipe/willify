Short Description

Released in 2020, the song is characterized by its upbeat tempo and engaging rhythm, blending pop and acoustic elements. The lyrics of "Mesin Waktu," which translates to "Time Machine" in English, explore themes of nostalgia and longing. Budi Doremi sings about the desire to turn back time to relive precious moments and correct past mistakes, resonating with listeners who share similar sentiments of reflecting on their past. The song's relatable message and memorable tune have made it popular in the Indonesian music scene.
