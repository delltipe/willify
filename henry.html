Short Description

The song captures the theme of late-night communication, particularly through text messages, often fueled by alcohol and raw emotions. Released in 2019, "Drunk Text" combines moody melodies with introspective lyrics, exploring the vulnerability and candidness that can come with inebriated communication. The song's relatable subject matter and emotive delivery resonate with listeners who have experienced similar moments of impulsive expression.
