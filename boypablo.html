Short Description

"Honey" is a song by Boy Pablo, a Norwegian indie-pop artist whose real name is Nicolás Pablo Rivera Muñoz. Released in 2020, "Honey" is part of the album *Wachito Rico*. The song features Boy Pablo's signature blend of catchy melodies, dreamy guitar riffs, and heartfelt lyrics. "Honey" revolves around the theme of young love, capturing the sweetness and innocence of romantic feelings. The laid-back, summery vibe of the song, combined with its relatable lyrics, has made it a favorite among fans and a standout track in Boy Pablo's discography.
