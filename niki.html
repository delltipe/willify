Take A Chance With Me

Artist

NIKI

Producer

Jacob Ray, NIKI, & Reske

*sound quality does not match purchased product.

Short Description

The song, performed by NIKI, explores unspoken feelings and the vulnerabilities of love. The lyrics express admiration for someone, inviting them to take a chance on a potential relationship. It's a heartfelt plea for communication and a call to be brave in matters of the heart. So, why not raise the stakes and embrace the possibility of something real and meaningful?
