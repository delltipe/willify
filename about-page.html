About Us

Willify is a renowned music streaming platform that made its debut in 2006. Originating as a digital audio service, Willify quickly gained prominence and has become a household name in the world of music.

Starting with a vision to revolutionize how people consume music, Willify has grown into a global phenomenon with millions of active users. A distinctive feature of Willify is its personalized approach, leveraging algorithms to curate playlists and recommendations tailored to individual tastes.

Our Founder

Elias Stromberg, born in Stockholm in 1980, combined his passions for music and technology to create Willify in 2006. With a background in computer science and experience in digital music, Elias envisioned a platform where users could purchase and download songs easily while ensuring artists are fairly compensated.

Under his leadership, Willify has become a leading digital music store, transforming how people buy and enjoy music. Elias is also a dedicated musician and advocate for digital rights and music education.
