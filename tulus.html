Short Description

"Hati-Hati di Jalan" by Tulus convey a message of caution and mindfulness in navigating life's journey. Through poetic imagery and introspective lines, the song reflects on the challenges, uncertainties, and risks encountered along the way. It urges listeners to proceed with care, acknowledging the importance of being mindful of their actions and surroundings. The song's gentle yet profound lyrics encourage self-awareness and responsibility, resonating with audiences who relate to the themes of resilience and personal growth.
